About the centre

A home for memorisation, wherever your home is

Darul Qur'an Centre runs a fully online Tahfidhul Qur'an program built for students all over the world. Our students live in different countries, work different hours, and start from different points in the Qur'an. That's why every student gets a teacher who follows their own pace, so a beginner and a returning hafidh can grow in the same program without either one being held back.

But memorising words is not enough on its own. That's why every student also learns Tajweed, so their recitation stays correct, and Tawheed, so they understand the meaning behind what they memorise. Add in basic Arabic, and the Qur'an stops being sounds to repeat and becomes a text you truly understand.

What's covered

One program, four foundations

Every class brings these four skills together. Memorisation is the goal, but the other three are what make it accurate, meaningful, and easy to carry with you for life.

Hifz — memorisation

You get a clear daily portion to memorise, plus time to revise what you already know. New memorisation never pushes out the old.

Tajweed

You learn the rules for reciting the Qur'an the right way, so your pronunciation stays correct as you memorise more.

Tawheed

You learn the core beliefs behind the words you memorise, explained in plain language for any age or background.

Arabic language

You learn to read Arabic and understand the basics, so the Qur'an becomes a text you understand, not just words you repeat.
